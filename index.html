Our Car

Our car is designed on the basic principle of simplicity. Quoting Jeremy Clarkson from The Grand Tour, S1E1, “You’re buying a car that was set up in a factory by engineers that know what they’re doing. And all you have to do is enjoy it.”

  • Our car’s design was based on Michael Keaton’s Batman, 1989. Our car has a front part that is intended to direct airflow over the wheels and chassis.
  • The front wings deflect air travelling towards the front wheels over the wheel, eliminating a potential high-pressure area. Downforce is created as the air underneath the car moves faster than the air above the chassis. The back wing is installed to provide better airflow.
